Bosco's Bones & Brews Menu Design

Menu Illustration and Layout | 01/2022 | Client: Bosco's Bones and Brews | Programs: Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign

Bosco's Bones and Brews is a restaurant with a lot of history. In researching the restaurant I found that their website had an old fashioned black and white style and I wanted to find a way to capture that in my design. The owner and I discused encorperating the building and from there I desided to create the building in an etching style. After getting approval from the client I went ahead and added a sepia tone and a paper texture. From there we worked on the fonts and I created the layout for the menu items that he can change at his leasure.
